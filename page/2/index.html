/Applications/Utilities/Terminal.app/Contents/Resources/Fonts/

在 Xcode 中提取 SF Mono 字体完整路径为：

/Applications/Xcode.app/Contents/SharedFrameworks/DVTKit.framework/Versions/A/Resources/Fonts
